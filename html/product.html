Camera Accesories and Equipment

Description
Experience the benefits of storing, transporting, and protecting your camera and an attached 150-600mm lens plus related accessories in this black Digital Holster 150 from Think Tank Photo. This holster comes with an included divider for stacking smaller lenses. Photographers will appreciate the quick-access zippered top opening, front zippered pocket, side pocket, stretch pocket, and inner lid pocket for SD cards or a lens cloth. The holster also features a monopod/small tripod attachment, and a seam-sealed rain cover. Carry the holster with the grab handle or wear it with the belt mount and included shoulder strap.
